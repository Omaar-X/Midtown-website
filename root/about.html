Our Story

Founded in 2010, MIDTOWN AABASHON LTD has been at the forefront of land development in Greater Dhaka, particularly in the Purbachal region. With over a decade of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the real estate industry.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide safe, legally verified, and well-planned land plots for families looking to build their dream homes. Over the years, we have expanded our portfolio to include various residential and commercial projects.

Our Values

Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We prioritize their needs and satisfaction.
